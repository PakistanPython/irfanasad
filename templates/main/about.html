Our Story

Founded in 1990, Alpine Limousine has been New York City's trusted partner for luxury transportation for over three decades. What started as a small family business with a vision to provide exceptional service has grown into one of the most respected names in the industry.

Our commitment to excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of corporate executives, celebrities, and discerning individuals who demand the very best in luxury transportation.

Today, Alpine Limousine continues to set the standard for luxury transportation services, combining traditional values of service excellence with modern technology and innovation.
