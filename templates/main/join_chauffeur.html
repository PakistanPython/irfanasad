Chauffeur Requirements

Basic Requirements

  • Minimum age: 25 years old
  • Valid driver's license (5+ years)
  • Clean driving record (no major violations)
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Professional appearance and demeanor
  • Excellent customer service skills
  • Fluent in English (additional languages a plus)

Preferred Qualifications

  • Previous chauffeur or professional driving experience
  • Knowledge of NYC area and major airports
  • TLC license (or ability to obtain)
  • Commercial driver's license (CDL) for larger vehicles
  • Customer service or hospitality background
  • Defensive driving course completion
  • Flexibility to work evenings and weekends
